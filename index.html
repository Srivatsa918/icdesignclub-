About Us

The IC Design Club is dedicated to fostering interest and skills in integrated circuit design among students and professionals. Our club focuses on providing hands-on workshops, expert talks, and collaborative projects that enhance practical understanding and experience in the field.

Our Events

We host a variety of events including design challenges, seminars with industry experts, and interactive workshops. These events are tailored to both beginners and advanced enthusiasts in IC design to help them expand their knowledge and network.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an engaging platform for students to explore and advance their skills in IC design. We strive to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application, preparing members for successful careers in the electronics and semiconductor industries.
